Diwali Celebrating Day

Diwali in the true sense means ending all sort of evilness, harshness, cruelty & hatred between one another. It simply means commemorating the beginning of brightness, joy, happiness & goodness in all spheres of life. One should start adopting positive habits & ideas from this auspicious festival and start removing all forms of negativity from life.
